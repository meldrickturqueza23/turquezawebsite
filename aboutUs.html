"About Us"

Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College

is a private education institution in Tumauini, Isabela. Since its foundation in 2005, the school has provided quality education that builds graduates who are competent, active, and adaptive in navigating problems in both local and global communities.

HG Baquiran College

is recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Department of Education (DepEd). The school mainly offers undergraduate programs in Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management.
